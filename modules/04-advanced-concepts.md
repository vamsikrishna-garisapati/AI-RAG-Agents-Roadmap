# Advanced Concepts

## GraphRAG
GraphRAG involves leveraging graph-based representations to enhance the capabilities of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) models. By integrating knowledge graphs, entities, and relationships, GraphRAG improves the contextual understanding and retrieval efficiency, leading to more accurate and relevant generation outputs.

## Memory Systems
Memory systems in AI systems play a crucial role in retaining information over interactions. Incorporating memory allows models to remember past interactions, making them more context-aware and personalized. This is essential for applications where continuity and user history are vital for performance.

## Production Deployment
Deploying AI models into production involves numerous considerations such as scalability, latency, and monitoring. Effective strategies must be established for deploying models efficiently in real-time applications while ensuring they can handle the expected load and continue to perform well over time.
